​Cliffe cleaning began from the owner, Renata working as an independent cleaner, but as customers grew to know and love her style of cleaning, so did the high demand for her services.
Due to this, Renata used her unique cleaning techniques to personally hire and trainee employees to uphold the high standards she had created.
At Cliffe cleaning, we promote a positive and hardworking culture where professional cleaners are valued, and a strong sense of mutual respect is promoted within all areas that we service.
